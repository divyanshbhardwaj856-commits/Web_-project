ABOUT

About Our Clinic

A One Stop Centre in your neighbourhood for all your healthcare needs

Our Family Clinic provides affordable and quality healthcare to rural and urban areas in India. The organization implements an innovative family and rural patient-centered healthcare model delivered through a chain of family clinics. We strive to make Our Family Clinic a place where patients can always feel confident that they are receiving the best care in a friendly atmosphere. By understanding what is most important to you and your family, we’re able to deliver better quality healthcare that is also convenient. Our Family Clinic offers primary care for individuals and families across all ages, genders, and diseases. We provide care for all members of a family during all phases of life - infancy, adolescence, adulthood, and senior years. Our Family Clinic revives the age old concept of a ‘Family Doctor’ who you can trust and rely on for all your healthcare needs, be it an advice, treatment or even a minor surgical procedure.

Who We Are?

At OFC (Our Family Clinic), we are dedicated to making quality healthcare accessible and affordable for rural and semi-urban communities across India. As a trusted network of primary healthcare clinics, we proudly serve underserved regions of South India, bringing compassionate, patient-focused care right to the heart of the community.

What We do?

At OFC (Our Family Clinic), we create a welcoming environment where patients feel confident in receiving the best possible care. Our mission is to blend modern healthcare practices with the warmth and trust of a traditional ‘Family Doctor.’ By deeply understanding the needs of you and your family, we provide high-quality, convenient, and personalized care that meets you at every stage of life.

Our comprehensive primary care services cater to individuals and families of all ages, from infancy to senior years, and address a wide range of health concerns. Whether it’s preventive health checkups, tailored advice, expert treatments, or minor surgical procedures, OFC ensures you have reliable support for your healthcare journey. We prioritize creating a space where compassion, trust, and top-notch medical expertise converge, making us your go-to partner for all your health and wellness needs.

Experience healthcare that’s built around you, close to home, and designed to provide peace of mind for your entire family
